The problem

Spreadsheets weren't built to keep people safe.

Allergens: Most kitchens run allergens out of a tangle of spreadsheets and memory. It works — until the day it doesn't.

Revenue: Don't price on instinct and hope the margins work out… Often they don't.

Staff: Many restaurants depend on one person knowing everything. That's not a system — it's a risk.

Compliance is non-negotiable

UK law requires every dish to declare all 14 allergens, accurately, no excuses. One out-of-date cell in a spreadsheet is a genuine risk — to your guests, and to your business. The margin for error is zero.

Margins slip away quietly

Ingredient prices move every week. Without live recipe costing you're guessing your GP% — and on tight margins, guesses are what close kitchens. You can't protect what you can't see.

Empower every shift

When knowledge is shared, any member of your team can step up with confidence. Built-in training and sign-off keep everyone sharp — so the kitchen runs smoothly whoever's on. A confident team is a resilient team.
